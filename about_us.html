Goal

TutorCloud offers four tutors with different teaching styles that can cater to a wider variety of students, allowing us to create bonds between the students and tutors. TutorCloud believes that in order to receive quality tutoring, there needs to be a bond between the student and tutor. We want our students to feel comfortable on asking questions during their one-on-one sessions and reach out after-hours if they need extra help. To make sure our clients take advantage of this feature, we designed a questionnaire to get a feel for the student and what type of approach would benefit them most. After its completion, we will assign them to a tutor that best fits their responses. After being assigned to their tutor, the student will gain access to the tutor's personal contact information and content.
